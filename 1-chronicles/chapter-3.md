# Chapter 3

<sup>2</sup> the third Absalom, son of Maachah daughter of Talmai king of Geshur; the fourth Adonijah, son of Haggith;

<sup>3</sup> the fifth Shephatiah, of Abital; the sixth Ithream, of Eglah his wife.
